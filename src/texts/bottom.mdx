##### Pomodoro
- _Dec 15, 2019_ | 👨‍💻 [codeSandbox](https://codesandbox.io/s/pomodoro-modal-1vqz0?fontsize=14&hidenavigation=1&theme=dark) - 🚀 [site](https://1vqz0.csb.app/)
- Built a Pomodoro Timer as part of the features for MoTo, a Momentum clone app. The Pomodoro contains basic play, stop, and reset functionalities. 
It has a short break of five minutes, a longer break of fifteen minutes, and a twenty-five minutes time block for work. The play button has a fill animation
that progresses as time comes to completion.
